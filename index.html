    How Do you Support Your Software?

    We provide unlimited application support via email for our customers. We endeavour to respond to every email within just a few hours of receiving it. Your emails always get answered by one of our friendly in-house support staff. If you need technical support after the first year, or wish to receive free program updates, you may purchase an optional yearly support contract. Please contact us for details.

    DO YOU PROVIDE FULLY INTEGRATED WEB SOLUTIONS?

    AJAT Software has a long track record of developing fully integrated web-based applications, such as supply chain management systems designed specifically for small business. We offer expertise in client/server application development using modern, recognized development techniques, and can provide advanced Windows front ends, or simplified web-based interfaces to existing data.

    Will I be able to move the software if I need to switch to a different computer?

    Yes, if you have the Professional Edition then you can back up your data, install the software on your new machine and then restore your data. If you have the Enterprise Edition then you simply install the software on a new machine and configure it to point to the database on your network.

    What technologies do you use?

    We always select the optimal technological “stack” to the project requirements. We rely on JavaScript, Python, and PHP programming languages. We use ready-made content management systems such as WordPress, as well as modern React.js and Django Python frameworks. We create visual mockups of projects using Adobe XD. We specialize in AWS cloud solutions and are certified in this area.

    What experience do you have in doing IT projects?

    Our team has completed and implemented several dozen market-oriented projects. We have competencies in programming, IT project management, machine learning, SEO/SEM, web analytics and computer graphics. Our skills and experience are not limited to code creation. We are able to provide our clients with a comprehensive web/mobile application development service - from the idea to the implementation and development of the finished product.

    Do you provide guarantees for completed projects?

    Yes. In the case of products covered by the guarantee, its length and detailed conditions are a separate provision of the contract. After implementation, we can also take care of the administration, maintenance, and development of the created product.
